FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our interior design services and process.

How long does a typical interior design project take?

+

The timeline varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project. A single room typically takes 4-6 weeks, while a complete home renovation can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

What is included in your interior design services?

+

Our comprehensive services include space planning, color consultation, furniture selection, lighting design, custom millwork, project management, and complete installation. We handle every aspect from concept to completion.

Do you work within specific budgets?

+

Absolutely! We work with various budget ranges and will create a design plan that maximizes your investment. During our consultation, we'll discuss your budget and provide options that align with your financial goals.

Can I see a preview of the design before implementation?

+

Yes! We provide detailed 3D renderings and our new Home Scape VR experience allows you to virtually walk through your redesigned space before any work begins. This ensures you're completely satisfied with the design.

Do you handle both residential and commercial projects?

+

Yes, we specialize in both residential and commercial interior design. Our portfolio includes homes, apartments, offices, retail spaces, restaurants, and hospitality venues.

What areas do you serve?

+

We primarily serve major metropolitan areas across India, including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, and Pune. For special projects, we also travel to other locations. Contact us to discuss your specific location.
